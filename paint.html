Painting With Passion

Computer Society of India, NMAMIT is back with another awesome contest. We present, "Painting With Passion", a drawing competition for the artistically gifted. 🎨 Art in any form from oil paintings and watercolors👩‍🎨 to acrylic painting and digital art🖥️ will be accepted. The submissions will be judged based on their uniqueness, quality (neatness, design, composition, color/tones), and overall concept.🖌️ ♦️Participants can submit their pieces to csi.nmamit@nitte.edu with the subject, Painting with passion - USN ♦️The last date of submission is 27/02/2021 The competition is open to all branches and the best ones will be rewarded!🤩 Along with that, all the participants paintings will be displayed in an offline exhibition for everyone to see.
