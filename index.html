CSI Student Branch 2019-20

Computer Society of India(CSI) started in our college NMAMIT,NITTE in the year 2007.It aims at imparting technical knowledge and ethical responsibilities in the students through all its activities.It encourages and motivates students to organize and volunteer in various workshops,events to instill in them a sense of responsibility,confidence and determination.It also aims at personality development.Hands on workshops are conducted to make the students experience the latest trends.The theoretical knowledge obtained in the classes is used for the implementation,hence providing the practical knowledge to all.To keep the students updated about the recent technologies, technical talks are arranged. Every possible attempt is made, to make the students equipped with the best of the technical knowledge available to groom them into a perfect engineer.
